Description

Geralt of Rivia - The Butcher of Blaviken. A Witcher from the School of the Wolf, Geralt hunts monsters for a living. He has supernatural powers due to his mutations, which every Witcher is subjected to as part of their training. A master swordsman, Geralt does his best to be alone, but destiny has other plans...

Appearance

  • Silver hair
  • Piercing eyes
  • Slim
  • Covered in battle scars
  • Rides a horse called 'Roach'
